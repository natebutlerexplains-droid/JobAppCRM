## Company Overview
TechCorp is a cloud infrastructure company focused on enterprise solutions. Founded in 2010, they serve Fortune 500 clients globally with reliable cloud services and cutting-edge technology. Known for industry leadership and innovation.

## Key Products & Services
- Cloud Platform (AWS-like infrastructure)
- Enterprise Security Suite
- DevOps Automation Tools
- AI/ML Training Infrastructure

## Company Culture
High-growth tech culture with emphasis on innovation and collaboration. Remote-first team spread across 15 countries. Known for giving engineers autonomy, supporting continuous learning, and fostering a culture of experimentation.

## Organization Structure
Organized by product lines with dedicated engineering, product, and sales teams. Engineering reports to CTO, product to VP Product. About 400 employees total, growing rapidly. Clear career paths and mentorship programs.
